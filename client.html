The pupils of Concord College have often experienced long journeys before arriving in the UK. Our drivers are there to help with luggage and ensure their arrival in the UK is relaxed and stress-free. We offer assisted check-in and named drivers for these young adults who may be experiencing long-distance travel for the first time. When returning home for the holidays, we ensure their journey begins on time by meeting them at Concord College – any time, day or night, so they arrive at the airport in plenty of time – and assisting with the check-in process, if necessary.

We like to make your holiday start even earlier by removing the hassle involved in driving to the airport or seaport. Worrying about fuel costs, parking and arrival times is a thing of the past. Instead, we strive to ensure that our customers relax and start to enjoy their holiday the minute they leave the house. Upon return from a holiday, it's equally important to maintain that relaxation and ease yourself back into normal life. Our taxi services assist with this by driving you back home, safely and swiftly.

Our Saga Holiday customers receive this entire experience, with our chauffeur-dressed drivers taking them to and collecting them from the UK airport or seaport, accompanied by a welcoming smile and ready to offer a helping hand with heavy luggage.

Shrewsbury School is one of the county's top boarding schools, providing education and pastoral care for mixed 11-18 year olds. We provide a licensed private transfer service for the pupils and staff of the school. We collect students from their boarding houses and transport them safely to their destination of choice, several times a year, at the beginning and ending of the school term.

Transport for Wales are one of the largest transport companies in the UK, operating train services throughout Wales and the borders. They understand travel and the importance of their drivers and guards reaching their desired location safely and in comfort. Because of this, they choose to use our services, every time. We're available to transport their staff at all times of the day and night so that they are refreshed and ready to start their work.

As we are the preferred provider for Transport for Wales, we provide taxis on a regular basis for customers whose journeys have been disrupted. By using our services, they can be sure that their customers are thoroughly looked after by a professional driver.
